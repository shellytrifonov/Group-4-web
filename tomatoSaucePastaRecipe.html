Tomato Sauce Pasta


Making time: 30 min

Level: Easy

Category: Main Dish

Nutrition per serving

  1. Calories: 300
  2. Protein: 20g
  3. Fat: 10g
  4. Carbs: 40g

  6. SaveOrder

Tomato Sauce Pasta is a classic Italian dish made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a rich tomato sauce. It's perfect for beginners or anyone who wants a quick, delicious meal!

  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
2. In a pan, sauté the garlic and onion in olive oil until golden.
3. Add the tomatoes and cook until softened. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss the pasta in the sauce and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
